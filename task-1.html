About

The intricate network of tunnel ways facilitates the swift and cost-effective transportation of both goods and people across extensive distances. The emergence of the steam engine during the Industrial Revolution played a pivotal role in advancing tunnel way infrastructure. However, modern advancements have seen diesel and electric engines predominantly supplanting steam engines in tunnel way operations.
